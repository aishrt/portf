About Suman Lata

Suman Lata is a seasoned People Development Coach, who believes deeply in people—people and people, because people have the power to break you or make you. She strongly believes that individuals are the true strength. She believes that every individual has a unique personality that can be developed positively through the right coaching and self-discovery at the right time.

With over 22 years of corporate experience in people and business development, organizational transformation, leadership hiring, and leadership development. Backed by a strong academic foundation with dual master’s degrees in human resource management (MHRM) and Computer Applications (MCA), she brings a rare blend of strategic thinking, human insight, and practical execution to her coaching practice. She has personally coached and mentored more than 2,000 professionals and promoters, enabling them to gain clarity, confidence, and purposeful direction in their careers, businesses, and lives. Her structured, stage-wise coaching methodology focuses on deep self-discovery by guiding individuals through powerful reflective questions—What? Why? and How?—that lead to sustainable behavioral and mindset shifts.

Guided by this philosophy, she has expanded her mission beyond professionals and promoters to also coach students in schools, colleges, and universities, believing that when young minds are guided early, it plays a vital role in shaping strong individuals and contributing to nation-building. Her sessions for students are designed to help them understand themselves and express their thoughts clearly. She guides them through self-discovery and building self-confidence, teaching them to handle the questions, emotions, and pressures of adolescence—from peers, family, and teachers, as well as their own self-imposed pressures. Ultimately, her sessions empower students to understand themselves, distinguish right from wrong, make wise decisions, and build a strong foundation for a successful future.

Previous Experience

With a wide range of experience across diverse industries and sectors, Suman Lata has worked with organizations ranging from small enterprises to large corporations with up to 10,000 employees. She has held strategic leadership roles including Corporate HR, Senior Management Team member, and Business HR Head, driving people and business transformation at scale. Suman Lata began her career as a Front Desk Executive and progressed to the role of Global HR Head in a multinational company. She has successfully coached and developed thousands of professionals, from entry-level talent to senior white-collar executives, as well as promoters and entrepreneurs, enabling sustainable business growth through self-development, people development, and AI-enabled capability building.

By working closely with promoters and leadership teams, she has guided organizations toward future-of-work readiness, embedding AI, automation, and skill-based workforce planning into people strategies. She has enabled people and organizations to define and achieve short-term and long-term goals, create a clear digital and people vision, and build a strong strategic roadmap, actively involving core leadership teams to deliver measurable business outcomes in a rapidly evolving business environment.
